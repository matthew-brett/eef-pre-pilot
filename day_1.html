Day 1¶

  • 14.00 - 14.45 - getting our data brexit; introducing the problem of proportions. Are there too few Brexiteers in the survey?. How would we decide?
  • 14.55 - 15.25 - another problem of proportions. If a family has 4 children, what is the probability that the family has 3 girls? A simulation.
